The Journey

High School Frisbee: 2018 - 2019

I started playing frisbee serious during my junior year of high school. Our team name was the Slarnegs, we were an ok tealym my junior year but come senior year we went to nationals and were ranked 25th in the nation. I was a captain of this team during my senior year.

Swing Vote: 2019

Swing Vote was my first club experience right out of high school, we went to a high level tournament called YCC right before college, was such a great time! Hope to be coaching a team this summer!

Sideshow: 2019-2023

Sideshow is my college team who I have been a captain on since May 2020, we are looking really good this year and I am so excited for all we will do!

Watchdogs: 2021

This past summer I played for a club team named watchdogs, it was my first time playing on an adult club team. Was a good intro into adult club and will be trying out for higher level teams next summer! Watchdogs made it to regionals after going 6-1 at sectionals. Check out our sick sectionals montage here!
